Cachapa

This beautiful Venezuelan dish makes my mouth water on sight. People describe the covering as a corn cancake and inside you can put chicken, pork, beef, lettuce, tomatoes, etc. You may customize the cachapa however you like. I personally just eat mine with lettuce, tomamtoes, and cheese with mayo-ketchup sauce.

Mangu

Mangu is every Dominican's greatest delicacy. We usually eat it for breakfast but it can be eaten at anytime. It is a combination of mashed plantains, salami, fried cheese, onions, eggs, and we top it off with avocado. You can adjust it as you like. I don't eat meat so I eat it without the salami.

NYC Pizza

You can never go wrong with NYC pizza. Even the mice in the subways can be found eating the pizza. A dollar slice is a cheap and effective way to feel satisfied in the afternoon. You can get topings but a plain cheese slice hits the spot.

Mango

Mangoes contain the sweet nectar of the earth. They remind me of the tropical landscape of my homeland. I usually dont cut it up in pieces and just eat straight from the fruit.

Passion Fruit

If you want to reach my heart in a quick and effective manner, get me a passion fruit. I love anything that contains passion fruit. When I lived in Dominican Repblic, we would eat the fruit with sugar. Passion fruit juice with evaporated milk and sugar is a delicacy, you will not regret.
