Biography

Born in the Bronx

I was born October 6th, 2001 in the Bronx, NY. I was greeted into this world by such a large family. I'm pretty sure even my current barber was there. My great-grandma from my mom's side gave me my first bath.

Dominican Republic

Soon after I was born, my mother brought me to the Dominican Republic where I finally got to meet my dad. We lived with my father's side of the family which consisted of three aunts, my grandma, and great-grandma. My grandma owned a salon and we all helped around the salon when we could.

I wouldn't trade my experience in the Dominican Republic for anything. There was a friendly culture. All the neighbors knew each other. The commuity would always be there for each other. And we definitely knew how to turn any situation into a god time.

Back in NYC

After much consideration, my mother and I decided to leave our family in the Dominican Republic and pursue a better life in the United States. We first moved to Brooklyn where three other aunts took care of me while my mom worked two jobs. We lived in a small one bedroom tenement wiith a shared kitchen and bathroom.

After a year of living in such conditions, the great-grandma that gave me my first bath called us and allowed us to come live with her back in the Bronx. I've been living in the Bronx ever since.

University of Rochester

I currently attend the University of Rochester. I've only spent a year in this magical place and I have grown so much. I am constantly pushed out of my comfort zone and challenged intellectually and for that I will forever be grateful.
